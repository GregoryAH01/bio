Megadeth is an American heavy metal band that was formed in 1983 by Dave Mustaine

Mustaine had been kicked out of Metallica after writing a third of third debut album and he became determined to outplay Metallica with his own band

1: Thier first album, Killing is My Business... And Business Is Good, was Mustaine taking his pieces back and playing them at higher tempos, and with more complex parts.

2: To keep themselves established, they then went on to continue their outplaying of Metallica with their second album, Peace Sells... But Who's Buying?

3: Their third ablum , So Far... So Good... So What!, was plauged with production issues and fighting among the bandmates, but overall the album was still well recieved by fans.

4: Having gone through court-ordered rehabilitation, Mustaine got a new lead gituarist and retured to create a 4th ablum, Rust In Peace, with a new style that fans enjoyed

5: With a reformed style and issues among the band members solved, their 5th album, Countdown to Extenction, was at that point their best.

6: For their 6th album, the band made a new studio by renting a warehouse in Arizona and converting it. The resulting album debuted 4th on the Billboard 200 and was their most popular and well-revieced yet.

7: Now beginning to experiment, their 7th album was a mix of classic material and new material. It debuted 10th on the Billboard 200, and while not as well-recieved as Youthanasia, it was still well-recieved overall.

8: For their 8th album, aptly named Risk, Mustaine decided to let another band member, Huff, have more control over the writing. This was a huge mistake and Risk was a massive failure.

9: Having now left Captiol Records and signing with Sancuary Records, Mustaine fired Huff and Prager to produce the album himself with their old style, not wanting to repeat the failure of Risk.

10: After the band having dissolved for a short time, Mustaine reformed the band with a rewened focus on their older style and went on to create their best piece of work Countdown to Extenction.

11: Following the success of The System Has Failed, Mustaine went on to create another old-school album. United Abominations debuted 8th on the Billboard 200, and went on to be very successful.

12: Their 12th album was yet again a classic style album. This still proved well for them and the album was successful. On their 2010 tour in addition to preforming the Endgame music, They played Rust In peace in honor of its 20th anniversary.

13: Thirteen was another well-recieved album, releasing at number eleven on the Billboard 200. On their 2012 tour, they played Countdown to Extenction for its anniversary and continued playing Rust In Peace.

14: For their next album, Mustaine decided to leave Roadrunner records and form their own record label, Tradecraft. Super Collider debuted at 6th on the Billboard 200 and was the band's most successful album since Youthanasia.

15: The production of thier most recent album has been plauged with one of the band members quitting, one dying, and a difficult time finding skilled replacements. However, they overcame this and formed a new performing group and released Dystopia to much success.
